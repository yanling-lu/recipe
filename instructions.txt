Russian Chicken, Mushroom, and Pineapple Salad

Step 1: Boil Chicken until done. Approximately 40 minutes
Step 2: Wash mushrooms and cut into small chunks. Set aside
Step 3: Chop onions and cook onions in oil and then add the mushroom from the prior step. 
Season with salt and pepper
Step 4: Once chicken and mushroom are done, start layering the salad. 
The layers of the salad are shredded light salted chicken for the first later.
Then add mayo, mushrooms, mayo, shredded cheese, mayo, and then pineapple in that order. 
Step 5: Enjoy on lightly toasted bread.
